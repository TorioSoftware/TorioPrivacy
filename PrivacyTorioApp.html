1. What information about the users do we collect?
Information that you provide us: We collect the information you provide when you use our Site/App and our
services, including without limitation, when you sign up for an account, scan a QR code, share your feedback,
submit a complaint, communicate or interact with us in any manner. This can include Personally Identifiable

through our App, receipts, queries etc. We store just first 6 (six) letters in a social security number, and the
whole social security number is stored hashed. We also collect information about how you use our services,
such as the content you engage with or the frequency and duration of your activities. We utilize encryption for
certain data.
a) Camera Permission: In order to scan a QR code, our App needs a permission to access your camera.
b) Information that we collect when you use the Site/App: We also collect information while you access,
browse or use the Site and our App. In other words, when you use our Site or our App, we are aware of your
usage of the Site/App, and gather, collect and record the information relating to such usage, operating
system, make & ID, IP address, device ID and type, device crashes, identifiers associated with cookies or
other technologies that may uniquely identify a device. This helps us in providing our services to you and
making improvements in the Site, App and our services, fixing errors and bugs.
c) Geolocation: The Site and App does not collect your geolocation without your permission. If you decline
permission for us to collect your precise geolocation, we will not collect it, but then you will not be able to
make the most of our services.
d) Payment Information: We don’t store any of your credit card information. However, we store a token that
uniquely identifies a card from a payment service.
e) Customer Care: If you contact our customer support, we collect all your interactions with our customer
support.
f) Good Judgment: We suggest that you exercise good judgment and caution while providing your personal
information.
